About

I'm Arooba, a self-taught painter working mostly in acrylics and mixed media. I've been making art for years, long enough that it's stopped feeling like a hobby and started feeling like a habit I can't put down. I never trained formally, so I learned the way most self-taught painters do: by making a lot of bad work, paying attention to what felt alive versus what felt safe, and slowly trusting my own hand more than the "rules." That process still shapes how I paint today. My work sits in expressive, figurative territory, I'm more interested in a painting that feels true than one that feels finished.

I work in layers: acrylic, ink, collage, whatever the piece asks for, and I rarely plan a painting all the way through before I start. Most of what ends up on the canvas is something I discover while making it, not something I decided in advance.This site is where that ongoing process lives, one piece at a time.

— Arooba Maryam
